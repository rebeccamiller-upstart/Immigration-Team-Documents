Sponsorship & Support — Minimum Requirements

The following outlines sponsorship and support categories available at Upstart and their connected minimum requirements. For visa category details, review the Nonimmigrant Work Visa Categories Guide.

Consistent and sustained performance is required for all sponsorship and support categories. Demonstrating ongoing contributions to your role and to Upstart is a baseline expectation. Failure to maintain consistent performance could impact eligibility for continued sponsorship or support.
Category Minimum Requirements Planning Guidance & Filing SLA Premium Processing
Candidates Not in H-1B Status
  • At least 1 year of valid U.S. work authorization remaining
  • Legal ability to start work by agreed-upon start date
  • F-1 candidates must have at least one H-1B lottery opportunity remaining
  • Day 1 CPT cannot be used to start employment or meet eligibility requirements
  • Dependent-based work authorization must be extendable
In most circumstances, candidates in this category can start work immediately.

O-1 (New Hire or Existing Upstarter)
  • Candidate demonstrates extraordinary ability (e.g., sustained acclaim, strong evidence across O-1 criteria)
  • Favorable case assessment by the Upstart Immigration Team and External Legal Team
  • Clear evidence strategy (publications, awards, critical roles, media, judging, etc.)
External Legal SLA: 56 business days from confirmed submission of Upstarter questionnaire and documents.
4–8+ weeks pre-filing for case building
Start dates are tentative until petition is approved. Recruiters and managers should plan for variability in evidence readiness, RFEs, and potential denials.

F-1 OPT & F-1 OPT STEM
  • Role qualifies as a specialty occupation (H-1B criteria) and is Upstart sponsorship-eligible
  • Candidate meets education and experience requirements
  • Valid work authorization documentation: unexpired EAD, STEM OPT extension receipt (180-day auto-extension), or commitment to timely OPT filing
  • Upstart will pursue H-1B sponsorship for eligible candidates with remaining lottery opportunities
External Legal SLA (STEM Extension): 21 business days from confirmed submission of all questionnaires and documents.
In most circumstances, candidates can start work immediately.
