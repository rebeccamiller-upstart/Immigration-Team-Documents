Permanent Resident (Green Card)
Sponsorship Policy

Guidelines for green card sponsorship paths, eligibility, and filing requirements at Upstart.

Sponsorship Policy
  • Upstart offers day 1 green card sponsorship to Upstarters in roles that qualify for immigration sponsorship or support.
  • At its discretion, Upstart may pursue multiple green card sponsorship paths (e.g., parallel PERM and EB-1A sponsorship, etc.) on behalf of a sponsored Upstarter.
  • Upstart reserves the right to pause or cease green card sponsorship at any time for any or no reason.
Sponsorship Categorization Eligibility

The U.S. residency system is built on a "preference" hierarchy. Categorization eligibility is based on the Upstarter's professional profile and role.

The External Legal Team and Upstart Immigration Team will review the credentials and role of a sponsored Upstarter and recommend the most advantageous permanent residency path(s).
EB-1A (Extraordinary Ability) Sponsorship Requests
  • At Upstarter or business request, Upstart will approve one (1) legal review of EB-1A eligibility within a rolling 12-month period.
  • If the External Legal Team determines that sponsorship under the EB-1A category is likely to be successful, per our EB-1A Sponsorship Eligibility Framework, the Upstart Immigration Team will approve the project.
  • Conversely, if there is no reasonable legal belief that EB-1A sponsorship will be successful, the request will be declined and guidance will be provided for how to strengthen the case.
  • Our Upstarters are eligible to revisit the request once their case has been strengthened, per provided legal guidance.
Adjustment of Status (AOS / Green Card) Filing Policy

Upstarters become eligible to file for Adjustment of Status ("AOS"; aka, green card) once their priority date becomes current. The External Legal Team will advise once an Upstarter is eligible to file their AOS.

To qualify for automatic AOS project approval, Upstarters must have received a "Strong Performer" rating in the preceding six (6) months.

EB-2 to EB-3 Downgrade and Interfiling Requests

Upstart supports a single request to downgrade from EB-2 to EB-3 or submit an interfiling request. This request will be approved after the Upstarter has a consultation with the External Legal Team and understands and acknowledges the risks, if any, of doing so.

Travel While an AOS Application is Pending with USCIS

To ensure compliance with USCIS requirements, Upstarters must be physically present in the U.S. at the time of the Adjustment of Status (green card) filing, and remain physically present in the U.S. until the original receipt notices are issued and the Advance Parole (AP) travel document is received.

⚠ Do not make travel plans while an AOS application is pending without first consulting the Immigration Team. Departing the U.S. before your Advance Parole document is issued may result in abandonment of your AOS application.
