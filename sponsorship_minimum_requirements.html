Canada Transfer Work Permit Sponsorship

Upstart only supports transfers to Canada in exceptional circumstances. Requests for transfer and work permit sponsorship will only be considered under the following conditions:

  • Exigent U.S. immigration circumstances materially limit the Upstarter's ability to continue living and working in the United States, as determined by the Upstart Immigration Team
  • The transfer is approved by the Upstarter's manager, department lead, HRBP Lead, and the VP of People Programs and Platforms
