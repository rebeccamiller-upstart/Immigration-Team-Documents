H-1B Transfer Document Checklist

Please provide clear, legible copies of the following documents to support your H-1B transfer petition.

The H-1B transfer process is a collaborative effort between you, Upstart's Immigration Team, and our partners at Corporate Immigration Partners (CIP) and Envoy Global. Our shared goal is to complete your onboarding efficiently and by your offer letter start date. The process typically takes 4–6 weeks from offer acceptance to your first day at Upstart. We use premium processing to expedite filings.

Within 24 hours of signing your offer letter, you'll receive an email from CIP confirming that your case has been opened and outlining next steps. You'll also receive an email from Envoy Global with a link to complete an online questionnaire and upload your supporting documents, detailed below.

Immigration & Status Documents
  • I-140 Approval Notice
  • All Form I-797 Approval Notices (current and previous)
  • Visa Stamp (most recent)
  • CBP I-94 Record
  • All I-20s
  • Color Copy of Passport (biographical page + expiration page)
Employment Authorization (If Applicable)
  • Previous EAD Card(s)
  • Color Copy of Current EAD Card
Education Documents
  • Bachelor's Degree Diploma
  • Transcript/Marksheet for Bachelor's Degree
  • Master's Degree Diploma (if applicable)
  • Transcript/Marksheet for Master's Degree (if applicable)
Professional Documents
  • Resume / Curriculum Vitae (most recent version)
Compensation Documentation
  • Payroll Paycheck Stubs (last 3 pay periods)
Travel Alert
You must be physically present in the U.S. at the time of filing. Share all travel plans to Upstart@immigrationlaw.com.
Additional Notes
  • Please ensure all documents are complete and up to date.
  • If any document is not available, notify CIP.
  • Additional documents may be requested as needed.
Important
  • Submit clear, legible copies of all documents.
  • Ensure names, dates, and other details are consistent across documents.
  • Incomplete or unreadable documents may cause delays.
